Research
Medical Image Segmentation

My primary work focused on developing Neural Network models that perform Medical Image Segmentation. I developed a patch-based sliding-window approach for segmentation of input images using a Convolutional Neural Network Model. The model was evalutaed on the following tasks:

  • Identifying Cancer Tumour Cells in Blood Sample
  • Identifying Plasmodium Vivax (Malaria) cells in Thin Blood Films
  • Identifying Tumours in Mammograms
  • Segmenting Red Blood Cells, White Blood Cells, and Platelets in a Blood Sample
Analysis of Adversarial Attacks

I performed a comparative analysis of attacks like Iterative Fast Gradient, DeepFool, Carlini-Wagner, and Jacobian Based Saliency Map on LeNet as tools to evaluate the robustness of the LeNet architecture.
Visualisation of Neural Networks

I performed a comparative analysis of techniques to Visualize a Neural Network as tools to analyze Neural Networks.
